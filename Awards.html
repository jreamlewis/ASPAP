National Awards:

Grand Senior Presidents Cup (2014, 2012)

This award is considered the highest honor given to a chapter on a campus with a large Greek community (11 or more IFC fraternities) and a chapter on a campus with a small Greek community (10 or fewer IFC fraternities).

Alpha Kappa Pi Gold Cup (2015, 2013)

This cup is presented for achieving exceptional recognition in all areas of the Annual Report, signified by earning at least 95 percent of the possible points.

Pi Phi Pi Silver Cup (2011)

This cup is presented for achieving significant recognition in all areas of the Annual Report, signified by earning at least 87.5 percent of the possible points.

Over 30 Louis Manigault Awards

In honor of our principle founding father, the Fraternity issues awards in eight categories: Recruitment, Communications, Alumni/Parent Relations, Ritual, Service/Philanthropy, Finance, Standards & Risk Management, and Scholarship.

Delta Beta Xi Award Recipients

Humberto Baquerizo '03 Bruce D. Di Biscegile [1995] William Severino [2000] Robert J. Owens [2008] The Delta Beta Xi Award is given for sustained alumni service to the Fraternity, generally over a period of at least ten years. While potential criteria for the award are as varied as our brotherhood, general criteria include years of service, type of service, and significant achievements. To be inducted as part of a class, a brother must receive at least 2/3 of the points possible from the judges (past Delta Beta Xi recipients) and must be in the top ten among all the nominees for that particular year.

Alpha Sigma Phi Hall of Fame

  • Anthony Arujo '12

Annually, the Fraternity recognizes no more than ten undergraduate men, typically of senior class standing, and inducts them into the Undergraduate Hall of Fame. The men inducted into the Hall of Fame are the Fraternity's most accomplished undergraduate brothers that have made significant contributions to better their chapter and our Fraternity.
