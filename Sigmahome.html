We are not your stereotypical college fraternity chapter.

We are one of the oldest fraternal organizations at NJIT, hearkening back to the day when NJIT was known as Newark College of Engineering. Our five values, Silence, Charity, Purity, Honor, and Patriotism, dictate our actions. Our brothers care about academic integrity and prowess, as a certain GPA is required to pledge, and we boast the highest average GPA of any fraternity in NJIT. Our members come from all different majors and academic backgrounds, as well as nationalities, beliefs, and ethnicities. Unlike fraternities depicted negatively in the media, we have a strict no-hazing policy. We believe hazing causes more harm than good. We believe that individuality within the Fraternity allows the member to grow and to ultimately better the man. We are firmly committed to our brotherhood wherever we go, as many members can attest to the fact that Alpha Sig is like a second family to them.
